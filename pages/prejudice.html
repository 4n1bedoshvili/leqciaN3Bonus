Pride and Prejudice

Release Date:November 11, 2005

Director:Joe Wright

Starring:Keira Knightley, Matthew Macfadyen

Genre:Drama/Romance

Synopsis: A spirited young woman navigates societal expectations and complex relationships in 19th-century England, particularly with the enigmatic Mr. Darcy.

Awards

  • Nominated for several awards, including four Academy Awards for Best Actress in a Leading Role (Keira Knightley), Art Direction, Costume Design, and Original Score.
  • Won the Academy Award for Best Costume Design (Jacqueline Durran).

Gross:Approximately $121 million

More Details

"Pride and Prejudice" follows the story of Elizabeth Bennet, the second eldest of five sisters, as she navigates societal expectations, love, and class distinctions in 19th-century England. When the wealthy and reserved Mr. Darcy arrives in their neighborhood, his interactions with Elizabeth spark misunderstandings and misjudgments.
