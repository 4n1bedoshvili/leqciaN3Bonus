More Details

"Little Women" follows the lives of the four March sisters—Jo, Meg, Beth, and Amy—during the Civil War era. The film explores their coming-of-age experiences, ambitions, and the challenges they face as they navigate societal expectations, love, and personal fulfillment. The narrative is presented in a non-linear fashion, interweaving the sisters' individual stories.
