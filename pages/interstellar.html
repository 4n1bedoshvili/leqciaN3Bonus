More Details

"Interstellar" is set in a future where Earth is facing environmental collapse, and a team of astronauts embarks on a space journey through a wormhole near Saturn to find a new habitable planet for humanity. The story explores themes of love, time dilation, and the resilience of the human spirit. The protagonist, Joseph Cooper, leads the mission alongside a team of scientists.
