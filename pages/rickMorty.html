Rick and morty

Release Date:December 2, 2013

Director:Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon.

Voices:

  • Justin Roiland as Rick Sanchez and Morty Smith
  • Chris Parnell as Jerry Smith
  • Spencer Grammer as Summer Smith

Genre:Animation/Sci-Fi/Comedy

Summary:"Rick and Morty" follows the adventures of an eccentric and alcoholic scientist, Rick Sanchez, and his good-hearted but easily influenced grandson, Morty Smith. Together, they embark on surreal and often dangerous journeys across the multiverse, encountering bizarre creatures, alternate realities, and satirical commentary on science fiction tropes.

Awards

  • The series has received critical acclaim and has won several awards, including a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Animated Program.

Gross:Approximately $56 million
